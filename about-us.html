Hotel7cepl

Hotel 7CEPL is located in the Andheri district in Mumbai, 1.7 mi from Chhatrapati Shivaji International Mumbai Airport and 4.3 mi from Powai Business district. Free WiFi is featured and free private parking is available on site.Rooms have a flat-screen TV offering satellite channels. Extras include slippers and free toiletries. There is a 24-hour front desk at the property. Room service is available. The hotel also offers car hire and a tour desk.

ISKCON is 2.4 mi from Hotel 7CEPL, while Prithvi Theater is 2.5 mi from the property. The property is 3.7 mi from Bombay exhibition center and 6.2 mi from Bombay Kurla Complex. The nearest airport is , 1.7 mi from the property.
